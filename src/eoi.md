# what we'll ask

A preview of the Adelaide Design Week 2026 EOI form, so you can prepare your responses before opening Tally.

## before you apply

Please review the following requirements before submitting an EOI:

<ul class="checklist">
  <li>You, or a representative for you / your project, will be available and responsible pre, during and post event.</li>
  <li>You have your own insurance.</li>
  <li>You have read and understood the terms and conditions.</li>
</ul>

## the form, step by step

<section class="preview" aria-label="Form preview">

<details>
<summary>1. How do you want to be involved?</summary>

- **Project** — a project with a defined outcome (exhibition, tour, etc.)
- **Collaborate** — I am a designer / studio that wants to collaborate
- **Host** — a venue to host an event or exhibition
- **Team** — I want to be part of the ADW team (volunteer / collaborator)
- **Sponsor** — I want to sponsor Adelaide Design Week

</details>

<details>
<summary>2. Applicant details</summary>

- Full name (first, last)
- Organisation / studio name (if applicable)
- Email address
- Phone number
- City / location
- Website / portfolio link
- Social media handles

</details>

<details>
<summary>3. Proposal overview</summary>

- Project / idea / venue / role name
- Short description (50–100 words)
- Detailed description (up to 300 words)
- Key themes, ideas, or focus areas

</details>

<details>
<summary>4. Format & delivery</summary>

Format: exhibition · installation · talk / panel · workshop · performance · open studio · other.

- Preferred duration / format details
- Ideal dates or scheduling constraints
- Stage: fully developed · in progress · concept stage

</details>

<details>
<summary>5. Venue & spatial requirements</summary>

- Confirmed venue? (yes / no — assistance required)
- If you are a venue: name, address, capacity, type of space, availability during Design Week
- Spatial / technical requirements (AV, lighting, power, install needs, etc.)

</details>

<details>
<summary>6. Team & bio</summary>

- Lead contact person
- Collaborators / team members (if any)
- Short bio (100–150 words)
- Relevant past work or experience

</details>

<details>
<summary>7. Support & needs</summary>

Support requested from Design Week: venue matching · promotion / marketing · production · technical · funding / sponsorship guidance · volunteers · other.

- Any accessibility considerations?
- Any risks or challenges we should be aware of?

</details>

<details>
<summary>8. Media & documentation</summary>

- Upload images / sketches / references (if applicable)
- Links to video / documentation
- Permission to use these materials for promotion (yes / no)

</details>

</section>
